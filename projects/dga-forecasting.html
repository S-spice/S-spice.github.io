Dissolved Gas Forecasting with CBAM-SIREN-LSTM

A hybrid sequence-learning model for forecasting dissolved-gas concentration in transformer condition monitoring.

Problem

Dissolved-gas concentration forecasting is important for transformer condition assessment and early fault warning.

Approach

Combined attention mechanisms, implicit neural representations, and recurrent modeling to capture nonlinear temporal patterns in gas concentration sequences.
